How to play: Use your touchscreen or W,A,S,D keys to move the tiles. When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one!

You can play the original version via http://git.io/2048., created by Gabriele Cirulli. Based on 1024 by Veewo Studio and conceptually similar to Threes by Asher Vollmer.
